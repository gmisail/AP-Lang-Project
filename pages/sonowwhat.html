Computer Science in Education

So now what?

If computer science is not integrated into schools, then how can you learn the skills of the future? First of all, you can join courses online that teach the basics of computer science. Many of courses are free-of-charge; MIT OpenCourseWare, for instance, has hundreds of free learning resources. Secondly, major universities often hold events that strive to educate high school students in programming, engineering, and electronics. These events, which are often free, give students invaluable instruction by college students or college professors. As a student, you have little control of what is taught at your school. However, you have an abundance of resources at your disposal to learn independently.
