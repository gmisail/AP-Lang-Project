Computer Science in Education

Why?

This website was created with the express purpose to inform about the current state of computer education and to offer possible solutions. Computer science is an emerging field with a high demand for jobs. According to Indeed.com, a popular job-search website, the range for computer science related jobs is between $48,000 and $113,000. Having computer science skills is not only helpful for the job market; computer science is an invaluable skill in an age where we are surrounded by technology. With computers living in the pockets of millions, being able to control computers and knowing how they work is vital.
