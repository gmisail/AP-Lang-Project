Computer Science in Education

Statistics

Compared to the national average, jobs in computer science generally have very high incomes. For instance, the average income for "Computer and Mathematical Occupations" is $89,810 according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. This income is $39,190 greater than the national average income of $50,620.
